<p><p>The Full-Closed Platform Screen Door market features two primary control types: pneumatic control and electric control. Pneumatic control systems utilize air pressure to operate the doors, offering reliability and minimal power consumption in various environmental conditions. Conversely, electric control systems rely on electric motors for operation, providing precise control and integration with automated systems. Both types enhance safety and efficiency at train stations, but their selection often depends on factors like infrastructure, maintenance requirements, and operational demands.</p></p>
