<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market is categorized into three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multilayer boards. Single-sided boards feature components on one side, making them cost-effective for simple applications. Double-sided boards have circuitry on both sides, enhancing design complexity and functionality for moderate applications. Multilayer circuit boards contain multiple layers stacked together, allowing for high-density connections and advanced electrical performance, making them ideal for sophisticated devices like smartphones and computers. Each type serves distinct technological needs.</p></p>
