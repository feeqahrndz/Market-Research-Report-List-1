<p><strong>The Soy Sauce Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brewed</li><li>Blended</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy sauce market is primarily divided into two types: brewed and blended. Brewed soy sauce is traditionally made through fermentation, giving it a rich flavor and aroma, often sought after by culinary enthusiasts. In contrast, blended soy sauce combines brewed soy sauce with additives such as hydrolyzed vegetable protein, offering a quicker production process and standardized flavor. The brewed variety typically appeals to premium consumers, while blended soy sauce caters to budget-conscious buyers and mass-market products.</p></p>
