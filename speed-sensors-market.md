<p><p>The speed sensor market comprises primarily two types: line speed sensors and angular speed sensors. Line speed sensors measure the linear velocity of objects, often used in conveyor systems and manufacturing to optimize material flow. Angular speed sensors, on the other hand, detect rotational speed, commonly used in automotive and aerospace applications to monitor engine performance and control systems. Both sensor types play a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and precision in various industrial and technological processes.</p></p>
