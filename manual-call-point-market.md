<p><p>Manual call points are vital components in emergency alert systems, categorized into wired and wireless markets. Wired manual call points connect directly to a fire alarm system, offering reliability and low maintenance, ideal for permanent installations. In contrast, wireless manual call points provide flexibility and ease of installation, eliminating the need for extensive cabling, suitable for retrofitting and temporary setups. Both types enhance safety by allowing individuals to manually trigger alarms in emergencies, ensuring quicker responses.</p></p>
