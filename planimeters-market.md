<p><ul><li>Polar Planimeters</li><li>Linear Planimeters</li><li>Prytz Planimeters</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Planimeters are devices used to measure the area of irregular shapes. Polar planimeters utilize a rotating arm anchored at a fixed point to trace the shape, providing precise area measurements. Linear planimeters employ a straight-edge mechanism to measure distances along the perimeter. Prytz planimeters combine elements of both polar and linear designs for enhanced accuracy and versatility. Other planimeter types may include digital or electronic models that offer advanced features like data storage and automated calculations, catering to various measurement needs.</p></p>
