<p><p>The optical fiber cleaver market is categorized into single-core and multi-core types. They are typically used in traditional networking setups. In contrast, multi-core cleavers cater to fibers with multiple cores, allowing simultaneous cleaving of several fibers for increased efficiency in data centers and advanced communication systems. Both types enhance connectivity and minimize signal loss, playing a crucial role in modern telecommunication infrastructure.</p></p>
