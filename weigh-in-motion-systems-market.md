<p><p>Weigh in Motion (WIM) Systems are advanced technologies designed to measure the weight of vehicles while they are in motion, typically on highways or at toll booths. The key benefits of WIM systems include improved traffic management, enhanced road safety, and optimized enforcement of weight regulations.</p><p>The Weigh in Motion Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing road traffic, the need for efficient toll collection, and growing concerns over road infrastructure maintenance.
