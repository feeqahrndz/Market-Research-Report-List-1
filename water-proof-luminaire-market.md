<p><p>The waterproof luminaire market is categorized based on Ingress Protection (IP) ratings, indicating the level of protection against dust and water. IP65 luminaires are dust-tight and resistant to water jets, suitable for indoor and outdoor use. IP67 offers complete protection against dust and temporary immersion in water, making it ideal for wet environments. IP68 provides the highest level of protection, allowing for continuous immersion in water, perfect for applications in submerged conditions, such as aquatic installations.</p></p>
